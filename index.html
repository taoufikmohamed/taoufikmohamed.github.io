About Me

I design and operationalize secure, observable, and scalable delivery platforms using GitHub and Azure DevOps—standardizing CI/CD, Infrastructure as Code (Terraform / Bicep), and container orchestration to reduce lead time without sacrificing governance.

My engagements typically include repository & pipeline migrations (SVN / Bamboo / Bitbucket → GitHub), environment baselining, compliance & policy automation, performance optimization, and shift‑left security (CodeQL, secret scanning, dependency risk management).

I partner with engineering and platform teams to replace ad‑hoc scripting with reusable workflow patterns and implement actionable monitoring—producing faster releases, lower operational noise, and higher resiliency.
